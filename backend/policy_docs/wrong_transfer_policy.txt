Policy: Wrong Beneficiary Transfer

If a customer reports sending money to the wrong beneficiary or wrong UPI ID, bank staff must collect:
- Transaction ID / UTR number
- Date and time of transfer
- Amount transferred
- Beneficiary account or UPI ID
- Customer account last 4 digits
- Whether beneficiary is known or unknown

Recommended action:
- Register wrong transfer ticket.
- Do not classify as fraud unless customer reports deception or unauthorized access.
- Mark as medium priority for follow-up.
- Initiate beneficiary bank communication process where applicable.
- Maintain clear record of customer statement and transaction details.
