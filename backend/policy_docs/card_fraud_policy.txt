Policy: Unauthorized Card Transaction Handling

If a customer reports a card transaction they did not perform, bank staff must collect:
- Card last 4 digits
- Transaction amount
- Merchant name
- Date and time of transaction
- Whether card is physically with customer
- Whether OTP was shared
- Whether transaction was domestic or international

Recommended action:
- Temporarily block card if unauthorized use is suspected.
- Raise card dispute/fraud ticket.
- Escalate high-value or repeated unauthorized card transactions.
- Advise customer not to share card number, CVV, OTP, or PIN.
- Maintain audit trail for all officer actions.
