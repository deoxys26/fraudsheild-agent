Policy: ATM Cash Not Dispensed but Account Debited

If a customer reports ATM cash not dispensed but account debited, bank staff must collect:
- ATM location or ATM ID
- Transaction date and time
- Amount debited
- Account last 4 digits
- Transaction reference number
- Whether receipt was generated
- Whether the ATM belonged to same bank or another bank

Recommended action:
- Register dispute ticket.
- Verify transaction status with ATM switch logs.
- Mark the case for review, not immediate fraud escalation unless suspicious activity exists.
- Inform customer that reversal investigation will be initiated.
- Maintain record of the complaint and resolution status.
