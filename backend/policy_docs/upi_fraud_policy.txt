Policy: Unauthorized UPI Transaction Handling

If a customer reports an unauthorized UPI transaction, bank staff must collect:
- Transaction ID / UTR number
- Date and time of transaction
- Amount debited
- UPI app used
- Customer account last 4 digits
- Whether UPI PIN, OTP, or device access was shared
- Whether customer clicked any suspicious link

Recommended action:
- Temporarily restrict UPI access if account compromise is suspected.
- Raise fraud complaint ticket immediately.
- Escalate high-value or repeated unauthorized debit cases to fraud/risk team.
- Advise the customer not to share OTP, PIN, passwords, or remote access.
- Maintain audit log of all actions taken.
