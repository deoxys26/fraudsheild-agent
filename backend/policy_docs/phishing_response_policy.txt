Policy: Phishing / Fake KYC / Social Engineering Fraud

If a customer reports clicking a fake KYC link, sharing OTP/PIN, installing unknown app, or losing money after a suspicious call/message, treat it as a high-risk fraud case.

Bank staff must collect:
- Transaction ID / UTR number
- Date and time of transaction
- Amount lost
- Beneficiary account or UPI ID if available
- Customer account last 4 digits
- Link/app/phone number involved
- Whether OTP, PIN, password, card details, or remote access was shared

Recommended action:
- Block or freeze affected digital banking access where appropriate.
- Escalate immediately to fraud/risk team.
- Create urgent fraud complaint ticket.
- Send alert to responsible officer.
- Advise customer not to delete messages, links, call logs, or screenshots.
- Do not reveal internal fraud detection logic to customer.
